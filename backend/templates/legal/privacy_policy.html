MyHomeBro (“we,” “our,” “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Platform or sign any Agreement.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Account creation: Name, email, phone number, password, and basic address information.
  • Contractor profiles: Business name, license number, skills, service area, portfolio images, and optional biography or company details.
  • Project communication: Any messages, photos, documents, or other content you send through the Platform’s messaging or attachments features.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage & log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, referring URLs, and timestamps.
  • Cookies & local storage: We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember preferences, and help secure your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

a. Platform Operations

  • Create and manage user accounts and profiles.
  • Generate Agreements between Homeowners and Contractors, including milestone schedules and payment details.
  • Process payments and escrow releases through third-party providers (e.g., Stripe).
  • Enable secure messaging and file sharing for projects.

b. Service Improvement & Analytics

  • Analyze usage trends to understand how features are used and where we can improve.
  • Generate aggregated or anonymized statistics that do not identify you personally.

c. Communication

  • Send account confirmations, security alerts, and required transactional emails.
  • Deliver invoices, milestone notifications, and Agreement-related updates.
  • Respond to customer support requests and questions you submit.
  • Send optional product updates or marketing communications (which you can opt out of).

d. Legal & Compliance

  • Verify identity when required by payment processors or applicable law (e.g., KYC/AML checks via Stripe).
  • Respond to subpoenas, legal requests, or government inquiries where we are obligated to do so.

3. How We Share Your Information

a. Service Providers

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe): To process payments, manage escrow, and perform anti-fraud and compliance checks.
  • Email and notification services: To send messages like confirmations, reminders, and status updates.
  • Cloud storage providers: To store files such as project photos, Agreement PDFs, and attachments.

b. Business Partners (with Your Consent)

If you choose to opt into optional services (for example, screening, promotions, or integrations), we may share relevant information with those partners solely as needed to deliver that optional service.

c. Legal Requirements & Protection

We may disclose information if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to:

  • Comply with any legal obligation or valid legal process;
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of MyHomeBro, our users, or the public;
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing related to the Platform.

d. Aggregated or De-Identified Data

We may share aggregated or de-identified information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you personally for research, analytics, or reporting.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, including to:

  • Operate and maintain the Platform;
  • Provide ongoing project history and Agreement records;
  • Comply with legal, accounting, or reporting obligations;
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.

When you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days), except where a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

5. Your Rights & Choices

a. Access & Correction

You can review and update your profile information at any time through your account settings. If you believe any information we hold is inaccurate, you may request that we correct or update it.

b. Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data by emailing privacy@myhomebro.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 30 days), subject to any legal or contractual obligations that require us to retain certain records.

c. Marketing Preferences

You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking “unsubscribe” in the footer of any promotional message. We may still send essential transactional or account-related emails.

6. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our Platform is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps to delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

If you access the Platform from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the United States or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. By using the Platform, you consent to this transfer and processing.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. Your continued use of the Platform after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

MyHomeBro Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2025

MyHomeBro respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Business and profile details
  • Agreement, milestone, and invoice data
  • Payment and escrow information (processed by third parties)
  • Communications sent through the platform

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the MyHomeBro platform
  • Create and manage agreements, milestones, and escrow payments
  • Facilitate dispute resolution and support
  • Send account-related communications
  • Send transactional SMS notifications related to agreements, milestones, escrow funding, disputes, and account security where users have explicitly opted in

3. Communications

You may receive transactional communications via email or SMS related to your account activity. SMS messages are sent only to users who have explicitly opted in. You may opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers (such as payment processors and cloud hosting services) only as necessary to operate the platform.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information.

6. Your Choices

  • Update your account information
  • Opt out of SMS communications
  • Request account deletion where permitted by law

7. Contact Us
